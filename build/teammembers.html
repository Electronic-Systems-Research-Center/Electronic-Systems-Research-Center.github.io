Dr. Ralph Gerard B. Sangalang

Dr. Ralph us an Assistant Professor III at the Department of Electronics Engineering, College of Engineering, Batangas University - The National Engineering University, Alangilan, Batangas City. He also serves as the Center Head of the Electronic Research Center (ESRC), STEER Hub. With a wealth of experience, Dr. Sangalang has held various leadership roles, including BS Biomedical Engineering Program Chair, BS Electronics Engineering Program Chair, and Chair od both College and Department Student Outcomes (SO) Committees. He has also contributed as a Department Book Committee Member and Senior Coach for ECE Quizzers. Dr. Sangalang's academic journey and professional contributions are marked by a dedication to research and Student success, making him a respected figure in his field.

Engr. Carlos Jhay A. Arroyo

University Research Associate, ESRC

Engr. Carlos Jhay A. Arroyo is a dedicated Instrumentation and Control Engineer from Balayan, Batangas. He currently serves as a University Research Associate and Project Technical Assistant at the Electronic Systems Research Center (ESRC) and Digital Transformation Center (DTC), STEERHub, Batangas State University The National Engineering University, and he is also a Lecturer I (teaching Data analysis, statistic etc.) at the Department of Electronics Engineering-BatStateU. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Instrumentation and Control Engineering from Batangas State University The National Engineering University in 2023. Additionally, he has a unit toward a Master of Science in Engineering Management and is now pursuing a Master of Science in Electronics Engineering at Batangas State University TNEU.

Engr. Ariel M. Rosales

UNIRA, Project Sinag

Engr. Rosales earned a Bachelor of Science in Mechatronics Engineering from Batangas State University - The National Engineering University, Alangilan Campus. His expertise lies in Industrial Automation, 3D Modeling, and Fabrication. After graduating, he joined Katolec Philippines Corporation in Laguna Technopark, Biñan, Laguna, as an Automation Engineer in the Production Engineering Department. However, after a few months, he realized his desire to explore a different engineering field. As a result, he resigned and pursued a Master of Science in Electronics Engineering at his alma mater. Currently, while pursuing his master's degree, he works as a full-time University Research Associate at the Electronic Systems Research Center under Project SINAG and serves as a part-time College Guest Lecturer in the Electronics Engineering Department under the College of Engineering.
