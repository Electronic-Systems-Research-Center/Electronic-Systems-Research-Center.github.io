About Us

The Electronic Systems Research Center (ESRC) is committed in providing technical support and working space for R&D activities related to the field of electronics engineering.

Center Mandate

The Electronic Systems Research Center (ESRC) is committed to providing technical support and working space for R&D activities related to the field of electronics engineering. The ESRC conducts research, provides custom experimental setups, and develops prototypes for various fields of research that involve electronic systems.

Research Projects

Marvel at the innovative projects made by the ESRC for social and technological innovation.

Project BARAKO

A which stands for Batangas Actions towards Revitalization of Kapeng Barako Industry, aims to develop an optimized method of propagating kapeng Barako seedlings.

Project SIGAW

A cost-efficient tsunami early warning system to serve as an early warning to high-risk coastal areas. Several SIGAW units have been installed in the provinces of Batangas and Quezon with additional units being developed.
