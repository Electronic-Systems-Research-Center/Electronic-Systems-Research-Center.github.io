What is ESRC built for?

Electronic Systems Research Center provides technical support for research and development related to the field of electronics. They develop prototypes for various fields of research involving electronic systems. Thus, they offer space and technical mentoring to researchers conducting development work. Lastly, they engage in the innovation of existing projects by collaborating with stakeholders.
